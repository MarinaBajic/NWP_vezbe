Paris
$1000
Paris is known as the "City of Light" and is one of the most romantic cities in the world. It is home to iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and Notre Dame Cathedral. Visitors can indulge in the city's famous cuisine, explore the charming neighborhoods, and stroll along the banks of the Seine River.

Barcelona
$1200
Barcelona is a vibrant city with a rich history and culture. It is home to the famous architect Antoni Gaudí's masterpieces, such as the Park Güell and the Sagrada Familia. Visitors can also enjoy the city's stunning beaches, delicious cuisine, and lively nightlife.

Amsterdam
$800
Amsterdam is a vibrant city that offers a unique blend of history, culture, and liberal attitudes. Its world-class museums, picturesque canals, charming neighborhoods, and outdoor activities make it a popular destination for visitors.

Venice
$1100
Venice is a city unlike any other, built entirely on water. It is famous for its romantic gondola rides along the canals, stunning architecture, and delicious cuisine. Visitors can explore the city's many historic landmarks, such as St. Mark's Basilica and the Doge's Palace.

Berlin
$900
Berlin is a city with a fascinating history and a vibrant cultural scene. It is home to many historic landmarks, such as the Brandenburg Gate and the Berlin Wall, as well as numerous museums and galleries. Visitors can also enjoy the city's lively nightlife and delicious street food.

Prague
$600
Prague is a charming and picturesque city that offers visitors a blend of history, culture, and modernity. With its stunning architecture, vibrant nightlife, and delicious cuisine, it's easy to see why it's a favorite among travelers. From exploring the Prague Castle to indulging in the city's famous beer, Prague has something for everyone.
